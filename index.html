OUR MENU

Masala Dosa
Talk about ‘South Indian’ food and the first thing that comes to mind, at least for most, is dosa. The craze of dosa is not only limited in south india, but north indians also crave for dosa as their first choice.
Idli Sambhar
If there has been one dish that has been a true ‘game changer’ of the way south Indian food is perceived and eaten in India it has to be the humble and ubiquitous idli sambar, a dish that relies on family traditions.
Uttapam
Those who love Uttapam, is always their first choice for the discerning and they claims that it gives a simultaneous experience of three different dishes: crispy outer layer like Dosa; insides are all soft like an Idli and cooked toppings gives you a feel of pizza. Mini Uttapams have also become very popular, usually served with sambar n coconut chutney. also wid tomato chutney or spicy green chilli & mint chutney as well.
